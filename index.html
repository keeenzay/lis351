Meet Your Professor

Learn a bit about who’s teaching CS 577 and their background.

Marc Renault

Marc Renault is a Faculty Associate in the Computer Sciences Department at the University of Wisconsin–Madison. Previously, he was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics & Computer Science at Mount Allison University in Canada. Before that, he was a CNRS postdoctoral researcher at IRIF (Université Paris Diderot – Paris 7) on the project Distributed Biological Algorithms, and a postdoctoral researcher in the Operations Research group at LIP6 (Université Pierre et Marie Curie – Paris 6).
